Assumptions / Warnings

Inputs loaded. Ready for calculation.

    Calculation Assumptions

    • Duty pumps only are used in hydraulic storage calculations.
    • Effective storage is taken between pump ON and OFF water levels.
    • Total pumping rate = discharge rate per pump × number of duty pumps.
    • Cycle time target = 60 / maximum starts per hour.
    • Storage calculation assumes one filling period plus one drawdown period per cycle.
    • Rectangular economical dimensions use the selected aspect ratio when both sides are not fixed.
    For preliminary sizing only. Final design must be checked by a qualified engineer.
